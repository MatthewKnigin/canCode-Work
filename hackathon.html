2022 canCode Teen Hackathon

Description

This is canCode's second ever hackathon! Join as an individual or part of a team of up to 3 middle or highschoolers. In this hackathon, you will be challenged to code a game in Scratch (a block-based coding environment) that can be taught to younger kids and an optional accompanying curriculum. There are many great prizes and trophies available for the winners along with canCode swag for everyone!
